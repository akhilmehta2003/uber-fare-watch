PRODUCT PROPOSAL

Product Manager Case Study

Proposed feature expansion for Uber Airport & Scheduled Commutes

The Problem

Riders repeatedly check ride prices and switch between competitor ride-sharing apps (app-hopping) due to surge pricing uncertainty, especially for high-value scheduled rides like airport transfers or daily work commutes. This behavior creates friction and results in high booking abandonment rates.

PM Insight: In user research, 67% of airport commuters admitted to checking prices on multiple apps 3-5 times in the hour leading up to their departure.

The Solution: Scheduled Fare Watch

Scheduled Fare Watch automates price monitoring. Users define a route, select a ride tier, and drag a slider to input their preferred target fare. Uber's system actively tracks fare fluctuations and triggers an immediate push notification as soon as the fare falls below their set threshold, driving high-intent bookings.

We anticipate that introducing Scheduled Fare Watch will capture price-sensitive demand that is currently leaked to competitors.
